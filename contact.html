Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 27-2-2025

At Castle High Value, we respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding the information you provide to us.

Mobile Information Sharing

Your mobile phone number will only be used to send SMS messages related to our services. We will never share your mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories of data shared, including opt-in consent for SMS messaging, will be used solely by Castle High Value and will not be sold or distributed to third parties.

SMS Terms and Conditions

Product Description:

By providing your mobile phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from Castle High Value related to property surveys, risk assessments, and underwriting support updates. This service will not include marketing or promotional content.

Message Frequency:

Message frequency may vary depending on the nature of the service and updates related to your property or policies.

Message and Data Rates:

Standard message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier.

Opting Out:

You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying with "STOP" to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive a confirmation message, and we will cease sending SMS messages to your number.

Help and Support:

If you need assistance or have questions about our SMS service, reply with "HELP" to any SMS message you receive, or contact our customer support team at support@castlehighvalue.com.

Privacy Policy:

Your phone number will be handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We do not share SMS opt-in consent with any third parties for solicitation purposes.
