1. SMS Terms of Service

When you consent to SMS updates, Castle High Value will send messages related to your insurance survey, risk assessment status, and customer support. We do not share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

Message Types

SMS will be used to enhance customer care for insurers and policyholders by:

  • Sending reminders for high-value home inspections
  • Providing real-time updates on risk assessment report status
  • Delivering urgent notifications about policy coverage gaps or documentation requirements

2. Message Frequency and Costs

You may receive up to 6 messages per week. Message & data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.

3. Opt-Out Instructions

You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by:

  • Texting STOP to any message you receive from us
  • Contacting our customer support team

After opting out, you will receive a confirmation message, and we will cease sending SMS messages to your number.
